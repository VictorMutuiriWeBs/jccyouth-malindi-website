Welcome to the JCC Youth Felowship Malindi!
Join us in our mission to grow in faith, serve our community, and build lasting friendships.

Mission

Spiritual Growth

To nurture the spiritual growth of our youth by providing opportunities for Bible study, prayer, and worship. 2 Peter 3:18

Community Service

To encourage our youth to serve their community through acts of kindness, charity, and social justice. 1 Peter 4:10

Fellowship and Unity

To foster a sense of fellowship and unity among our youth, creating a supportive and loving community.

Leadership Development

To develop the leadership skills of our youth, preparing them to be future leaders in the church and community. 1 Timothy 4:12

Evangelism and Outreach

To equip our youth to share the Gospel and reach out to their peers with the love of Christ. Matthew 28:19

Personal Development

To support the personal development of our youth, helping them to discover and use their God-given talents and abilities. Jeremiah 29:11

Vision

Leading Community Volunteers

Empowering youth to lead community service initiatives, fostering compassion and positive change in society.

Educating Future Leaders

Providing educational resources and opportunities to equip youth with knowledge and skills for a brighter future.

Spiritual Growth for Youth

Guiding youth in their spiritual journey, nurturing faith and fostering a deeper relationship with God.

Global Gospel Outreach

Inspiring youth to spread the Gospel worldwide, sharing Christ’s love and message with all nations.
